網站理念



武漢肺炎（COVID-19）於 2019 年底在中國爆發，短短數個月內肆虐全球，臺灣因為與中國地理位置鄰近，民間往來密切，因此曾被預測將是中國以外疫情最嚴重國家。但是，臺灣不但有效控制住疫情，將對民眾的影響降到最低，臺灣的防疫工作成果與模式更受到國際社會廣泛的關注與肯定。身為一個小小的學生希望也可以盡一份心力，因此設計這個網站《Covid19資訊網》，希望能讓國人在蒐集武漢肺炎相關資訊時能事半功倍，同時也能與分享臺灣是如何有效防堵疫情的擴散，並在行有餘力時進行國際人道救援，協助國際社會共同抗疫。

Wuhan pneumonia （COVID-19）outbreak in China at the end of 2019, which has ravaged the world in just a few months.Taiwan, which has been predicted to be the worst affected country because of its close proximity with China and close people-to-people contacts. However, Taiwan not only effectively control the epidemic but also minimize the impact on the people, Taiwan's epidemic prevention work results and models by the international community more widely concerned and affirmed.As a student, we hope that we can also do a part, so we design of this website "Covid-19 Information Website", hope that the Taiwaness people in the collection of Information about Wuhan pneumonia can do more with less, but also share how Taiwan is effective in preventing the spread of the epidemic, and in the past efforts to carry out international humanitarian relief, to assist the international community to fight the epidemic together.
